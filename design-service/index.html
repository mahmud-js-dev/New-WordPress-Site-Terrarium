Our Process

Get to know you and your specific needs


Terradium is more than terrarium designers. We don’t just stick the same terrarium in every space we work with. We take the time to get to know you, your needs, your brand – we want to understand who you are and what you do. Then, we create custom solutions based on your unique needs to bring your space to life.

Our plants aren’t just green — we have a full-color scope at our fingertips to match your color palette. We’ll find the best locations to maximize your look, vibe, and mood. Terradium designs and builds contained gardens of creativity, productivity, and growth. Take us around your space, let’s work together to create something magical.

Build it custom to fit


We’ll come back! We know the biggest concern for the people we work with is “now what do we do, who can we keep this looking so nice?” But don’t worry — we’re here to help. When you work with terradium we will be there, for all the steps; we’re not going to just show up, drop off some terrariums, and leave it to you to figure it out.

We are a true partner and will provide ongoing maintenance to keep your terrariums alive and thriving. We have friendly, professional, educated service specialists who know how to keep your terrariums in tip-top shape.

We can even hook you up with terrarium swap service where we can switch out the terrariums on a regular basis to keep it fresh and eye-catching. We customize our maintenance service so it works with you.
